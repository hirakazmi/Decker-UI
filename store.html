CME

 SAM offers CME for internists in all circumstances. SAM CME exercises are post-tests, associated with the monthly SAM topic updates. Each exercise comprises case-based MCQs, assessing new material and knowledge gaps for each topic. All exercises and MCQs are 'Board-style', helping physicians prepare for the re-certification exam one month at a time!
